Cluster Chart

Description

Usage Case

Cluster charts are a chart type that displays hierarchical data, and orders them based on whether they are a root, parent, child, or leaf node. Root meaning that they have no parent nodes, and leaf meaning that they have no children nodes. All roots and leaves are ordered on the same level, and the middle levels are differentiated through how long the chain of parent/children are. Add interaction or labeling.

Marks

2 - Points for each node, and connections for their children

Channels

0 - No real channels differentiating them at all for this chart.

Some potential channels could include color for differentiating the each node location in the cluster, or the area of the node to denote how important it is.
