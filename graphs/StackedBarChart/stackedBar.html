Stacked Bar Chart

Description

Usage Case

Stacked bar charts tend to show comparisons between different categories of data, but they use colors to display the proportion of specific data points that make up the whole. It is relatively easy to compare the sizes of different bars and notice the differences between them, so this is a popular means of displaying a comparison. In this case, the bar chart is used to show the proportion of food servings eaten per person, and the colors split up food groups.There should be a legend denoting what each color represents as well.

Marks

2 - Line, as each section of the bar chart is a thick line.

Channels

3 - Horizontal and vertical positions, and color to differentiate each flavor.
