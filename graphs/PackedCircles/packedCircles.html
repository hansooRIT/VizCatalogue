Packed Circle Graph

Description

Usage Case

Packed circle graphs display hierarchical data, and shows the hierarchy of each node through a series of circles within circles. The largest circle being the root node, and each smaller circle being children of that root. The larger the circle is, the more children they have. Add legend or labels.

Marks

2 - Point for each circle, and containment to denote parent/child relationship

Channels

1 - Color. to denote different nodes in the hierarchy.

Another potential channel could be color, but it is not in this case as the color is uniform.
