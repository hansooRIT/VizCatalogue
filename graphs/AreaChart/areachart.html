Area Chart

Description

Usage Case

Area charts tend to describe a time-series relationship. What this means is that it wants to show a change in some sort of observable data over a period of time using the area as a visual indicator. In this case, the area chart is used to show a change in calorie intake per day of the week for a person.

Marks

1 - Area

Channels

2 - Horizontal and vertical positions

Another potential channel could be color, but it is not in this case as the color is uniform.
