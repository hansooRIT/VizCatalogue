Partitions

Description

Usage Case

Partitions visualize hierarchical data with a series of rectangles all attached to their parent nodes. The root node has a connection to all of their children, and they are those with more children are bigger.

Marks

1 - Area to denote number of children

Channels

2 - Area and spatial location to denote parent/child relationship.

Another potential channel could be color, if it is chosen to display a node's location in the hierarchy.
